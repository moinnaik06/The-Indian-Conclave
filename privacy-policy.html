Privacy Policy

The privacy policy of the Indian Conclave (hereinafter referred to as “Privacy Policy”) provided below is with respect to our use and protection of any personal information you provide to us through The Indian Conclave Website (hereinafter referred to as “Website”). You may be required to provide personally identifiable information at several different points on our Website such as the registration forms in the Events Tab, the Log-In Protocol for accessing the Articles section and Contacting Us via emails. By accepting the policy at the time of registration the user expressly approves and consents to our collection, storage, use and disclosure of personal information as described in this Privacy Policy.

1. The Indian Conclave (hereinafter referred to as “TIC”) respects the privacy of its users and is committed to protecting the personally identifiable information (any information or data by which you can be identified, such as names, addresses, zip/postal codes, email addresses, telephone numbers, credit or debit card details, etc.) that the users share with us, in all respects. To better protect your privacy, we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. We assure that we will take steps to use your personal information only in ways that are compatible with this Privacy Policy.

2. In general, you can visit the Website without telling us who you are or revealing any personal information about yourself. Please keep in mind that whenever you voluntarily disclose any personal information online, for example through e-mail, submissions, Google Forms or elsewhere, that information can be collected and used by our Data Analysis Team for understanding the trends of our users and their demographics as explained further in point 4.

3. TIC feels strongly about protecting the privacy of children and encourages children to involve their parents in any interactive activity they participate in online. If you are under 16, TIC encourages you to use this Website only with the help and in the presence of your parents or guardians of legal age. However, please note that it is a mere suggestion.

4. TIC may use the information in its databases, including personal information you’ve submitted to analyze the site and user behavior, troubleshoot technical problems, resolve disputes and address complaints, and prepare aggregated reports that are shared with other stakeholders, including the Board of Directors of TIC.

5. There are times when we may collect personal information from you such as name, emails, physical address and/or telephone number for staying in touch with you and contacting you for the event. Generally, you will have the option in the future to be able to “opt out” of certain uses of the information. If you choose not to provide the information we request, you can still visit TIC, but it may hamper your access to certain events and services which require those details.

6. From time to time, we may conduct online surveys or run a contest for promotional purposes, during which, we may ask users for contact information (like email addresses) and demographic information (like gender, age, or region). We use contact data to notify people if they win the contest or wish to participate in ongoing ones. We understand, though, that you may want to participate in these surveys or contests but may not want us to send you an email. Accordingly, as part of these surveys or contests, we will always give you the option of declining to receive information from TIC which can be done by clicking the unsubscribe option in the mail itself.

7. TIC contains many links to other Web sites. For instance, we use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Website. These companies may use information about your visits to this and other Websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. Such third party sites are governed by their respective privacy policies and TIC is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Websites. Once you leave our servers, use of any information you provide is governed by the Privacy Policy of the operator of the site you are visiting. That policy may differ from ours. If you cannot find the Privacy Policy of any of these sites via a link from the site’s homepage, you should contact the site directly for more information. TIC does not share any of the individual personal information you provide with the sites to which TIC links, although TIC may share basic aggregate data with such sites (such as how many people use our site). This includes other third party sites on the Internet for which TIC provides search services.

8. TIC will take appropriate steps to protect the information you share with us. We have implemented technology and security features and strict policy guidelines to safeguard the privacy of your personally identifiable information from unauthorized access and improper use or disclosure. TIC will continue to enhance its security procedures as new technology becomes available and stronger domain features get established.

9. All information gathered on TIC is securely stored within the TIC (controlled) database. The database is stored on servers secured behind a firewall; access to the servers is password protected and is strictly limited only to the Technical Team of TIC. However, as effective as our security measures are, no security system is impenetrable. We cannot guarantee the security of our database, nor can we guarantee that information you supply will not be intercepted while being transmitted to us over the Internet.

What is The Indian Conclave : Operation Indradhanush ?

The Indian Conclave is a Youth Organisation run majorly by students who are under the age of 18 and this organization largely works on conducting a plethora of activities pertaining to capacity building, personality development, leadership cultivation and cultural exchange programs for the youth.

The idea of TIC was born amidst the initial days of the pandemic when schools were shut and activities had ceased. The concept emerged with a motive to connect the Youth of the globe through multiple simulations that promote academic and extracurricular interests. We take pride in simulating events such as Youth Parliaments, Model United Nations conferences, Parliamentary debates and interaction sessions with eminent personalities across the spectrum.

In this edition we have our theme as Indradhanush (Rainbow), wherein we strive to conduct events focusing on LGBTQ+ community.As always we shall never compromise on our quality and shall thrive to provide our participants the best possible experience.
